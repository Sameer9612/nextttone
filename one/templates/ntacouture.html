About this course

NTA COUTURE is our most comprehensive 3-month professional modeling training program that covers all modules of our curriculum. This complete course includes full portfolio development, professional comp card production, and comprehensive career planning with international orientation. Top-performing students receive the prestigious NTA Quality Seal, marking them as industry-ready professionals.

The program encompasses all foundational and advanced skills, including advanced posing techniques, professional portfolio photoshoots, comp card design and production, contracts and industry awareness training, mock auditions, and a final runway presentation. Students receive comprehensive career planning guidance, international modeling opportunities orientation, and upon completion, full certification with the coveted NTA Quality Seal for exceptional performers.
