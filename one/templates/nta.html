Our Courses

NTA ESSENCE

NTA ESSENCE is your gateway to the modeling industry. This comprehensive 1-month foundational training program is designed specifically for beginners who want to develop essential modeling skills. Our course covers all the fundamentals including posing techniques, runway walking, camera presence, and personal branding strategies. You'll complete Module 1 of our professional modeling curriculum and graduate with confidence, skills, and industry-ready materials.
  • Click here For More Details

NTA VISION

NTA VISION is our comprehensive 2-month intermediate modeling training program that builds upon foundational skills with advanced techniques. This course includes all features from our ESSENCE program plus professional photography training, visagism analysis, casting simulations, and acting for camera. You'll complete Modules 1 and 2 of our professional modeling curriculum, preparing you for advanced industry opportunities.
  • Click here For More Details

NTA COUTURE

NTA COUTURE is our most comprehensive 3-month professional modeling training program that covers all modules of our curriculum. This complete course includes full portfolio development, professional comp card production, and comprehensive career planning with international orientation. Top-performing students receive the prestigious NTA Quality Seal, marking them as industry-ready professionals.
  • Click here For More Details

NTA CONNECT

NTA CONNECT is our flexible self-paced online modeling program designed for students who need maximum flexibility or want to access our training from anywhere in the world. This digital program covers the online version of Module 1 with comprehensive theory-based lessons and foundational studies. Perfect for busy schedules or remote learning needs.

Focused Training Course
1
Posing Techniques for Beginners

Makeup and Skin Care for Models
