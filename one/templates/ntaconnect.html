About this course

NTA CONNECT is our flexible self-paced online modeling program designed for students who need maximum flexibility or want to access our training from anywhere in the world. This digital program covers the online version of Module 1 with comprehensive theory-based lessons and foundational studies. Perfect for busy schedules or remote learning needs.

The program includes digital lessons covering modeling fundamentals, foundational studies, online support and guidance from our expert instructors, and certification upon successful completion. Please note that this online program does not include portfolio development or showcase components - it focuses purely on theoretical knowledge and foundational concepts that can be learned remotely.
