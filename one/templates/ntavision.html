About this course

NTA VISION is our comprehensive 2-month intermediate modeling training program that builds upon foundational skills with advanced techniques. This course includes all features from our ESSENCE program plus professional photography training, visagism analysis, casting simulations, and acting for camera. You'll complete Modules 1 and 2 of our professional modeling curriculum, preparing you for advanced industry opportunities.

The program includes advanced posing techniques, styling consultation, skincare and nutrition guidance, wellness coaching, and comprehensive audition preparation. Students receive professional photoshoots, casting simulation sessions, and commercial presence training. Upon completion, you'll have a complete professional portfolio, industry certification, and the skills to excel in competitive modeling environments.
