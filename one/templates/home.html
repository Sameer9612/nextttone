FAQ

Have more questions about our modelling agency? Find answers to common queries below.
Image from

How can I apply to become a model? ⌄
You can apply by filling out our online application form and submitting recent photos. After reviewing, we will contact shortlisted applicants for interviews and test shoots.
What types of models do you represent? ⌄
We represent models for fashion, commercial, plus-size, fitness categories. We welcome diversity in age, background, and style.
Do I need prior experience to join? ⌄
No prior experience is required. We look for attitude and potential and provide guidance and training for new talent.
Does your agency charge any fees? ⌄
We do not charge sign-up or hidden fees. If you wish, you can purchase a professional portfolio package through us, but this is not mandatory.
How do you promote your models? ⌄
We promote our models through our website, industry contacts, casting platforms, and social media to maximize exposure and opportunities.
